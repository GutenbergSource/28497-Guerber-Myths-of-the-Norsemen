This project provides fast formatting feedback to F1 formatters, by way of diffs. Your careful work in F2 is appreciated.



This is a P3 Qualifying project. In P2 it is restricted to volunteers who have applied, or are thinking of applying, for access to P3, and it should be proofed according to P3 standards, including spellchecking.

In P2 please limit yourself to 15-20 pages per Qual. project.

Another book with old Icelandic myths and sagas, but this time all in English, so if proofing the old-Icelandic in Origines Islandicae is too much for you, this English translation gives you an easier alternative.

Please note the special characters used to indicate pronunciation in the index, such as macrons and over dots. Examples:

a with over dot [.a]

o with macron [=o]

double o with macron (over both) [=oo]

e with tilde [~e]
